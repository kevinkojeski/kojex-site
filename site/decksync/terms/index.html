2. Description of service

DeckSync is a presentation synchronization app that connects nearby devices over your local network using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework. Depending on conditions, data may travel over Bluetooth LE, peer-to-peer Wi-Fi, or the local Wi-Fi network your devices share. All paths stay local. The App requires no internet connection for its core features and has no backend servers.
